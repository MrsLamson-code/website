Quokka Routines

 Time of Day Activities 

Nighttime

Time to eat!

Quokkas are nocturnal, which means they feed by night, browsing for food and moving through tall grass quietly.

Morning

Nap Time!

Morning time is the time to take a nap. Quokkas like to shelter in the shade of trees, and are known to use the same resting spot every day.

Noontime

Restless!

Quokkas typically get along, but on occasion they may fight, especially to get treats from human visitors and to get the best napping spot on a hot day.

Evening 

Back to napping, it's hot out!

The quokkas is capable of bearing high temperatures up to 110 F, due to its thermoregulatory abilities. However, it can still get dehydrated.
