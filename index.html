Quokkas

The Happiest Animal 

on the Planet

On a magical island off of western Australia, there lives a colony of quokkas. Quokkas are small marsupials that weigh about 5-11 lbs and are about 16-21 inches long. They have a 10 inch tail.

They are about the same size as a small cat, and resemble a very small kangaroo. They are perpetually smiling, therefore earning them the nickname of the "Happiest Animal on the Planet".
